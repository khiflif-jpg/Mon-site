CDBG is based in Bitam, Northern Gabon

Located in northern Gabon, Bitam is the heart of our forestry operations. This biodiversity-rich region allows us to develop sustainable exploitation on 10,000 hectares of private property.

About us

10,000 hectares

of privately-owned land sustainably managed in Bitam, northern Gabon.

Tropical woods

Selection and trade of noble species: Okoumé, Azobé, Kevazingo, Padouk, Ebony, Moabi, Tali, Iroko, Movingui, Bilinga...

Local employment

Development of a sawing activity with local labor to strengthen Bitam's economy.

Our ambition

Master the entire value chain of Gabon's wood, from forest to international export.
