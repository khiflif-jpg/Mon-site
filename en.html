About CDBG

The Compagnie Durable du Bois (CDBG) is a forestry company in Gabon dedicated to responsible forest management. Our mission is to combine economic development with environmental stewardship through sustainable and certified practices.

CDBG operates from Bitam, in northern Gabon

Bitam, located in northern Gabon, is the heart of our forestry operations. This biodiversity-rich region enables us to practice sustainable forest management across 10,000 hectares of privately owned land.
