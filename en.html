CDBG is based in Bitam, Northern Gabon

Located in northern Gabon, Bitam is the heart of our forestry operations. This biodiversity-rich region allows us to develop sustainable logging on 10,000 hectares of private land.

About us

Expertise serving Gabon's forests

10,000 hectares

of private land sustainably managed in Bitam, northern Gabon.

Tropical woods

Selection and trade of noble species: Okoumé, Azobé, Kevazingo, Padouk, Ebony, Moabi, Tali, Iroko, Movingui, Bilinga...

Local employment

Development of sawing activities with local labor to support Bitam's economy.

Our ambition

To master the entire value chain of Gabonese wood, from the forest to international export, combining economic efficiency, positive social impact, and deep respect for the environment.

Our commitments

Respect for the environment

Our project puts ecology, fauna and flora at the heart of every action, for responsible, sustainable and engaged forestry.

Socio-economic development

Job creation and socio-economic development for the population of the Bitam region are at the core of our priorities.

Value chain mastery

Our ambition is to control the entire wood value chain from the forest to international export.

Collaborations and Partnerships

Our company, committed to sustainable wood management, is open to any collaboration or partnership request in logging, sawing, logistics and transport.
